# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the BengoBox Treasury project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Positive behaviour includes:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Respecting differing opinions and experiences
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behaviour includes:

- Sexualised language or imagery, unwelcome advances
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers clarify and enforce standards and may remove or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Enforcement

Report abusive behaviour to [security@bengobox.com](mailto:security@bengobox.com). Complaints will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement Guidelines

1. **Correction** – Private communication clarifying expectations
2. **Warning** – Official warning with consequences for continued behaviour
3. **Temporary Ban** – Temporary removal from community spaces
4. **Permanent Ban** – Permanent removal for repeated or severe violations
